How it works

Private by default

Every entry you write lives in your vault first. Nothing is shared until you choose to post it. Draft, revise, or just observe — the record is yours.

Private

The bloom tells you when

Each petal is something a member made public, placed at the hour it happened, in the colour of that hour. The bloom tells you when and how often — and never what.

You are looking at the cooperative’s rhythm. No account required to observe it.
